Sitong Liu, Illustrator & Visual Designer

About

Sitong is an illustrator/Visual Designer based in U.S. She loves to work with mythical creatures and dreamlike landscape for people who still believes in them. In addition to 2D media, she plays with 3D and augmented reality, challenging the ways stories can be told.

Grew up in China, now based in New York. Constantly looking for fun collaborations and projects!
