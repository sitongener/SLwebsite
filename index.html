About

Sitong is an illustrator/Visual Designer based in U.S. She loves to work with mythical creatures and dreamlike landscape for people who still believes in them. In addition to 2D media, she plays with 3D and augmented reality, chanlleging the ways stories can be told.

Grow up in China,she is now lived and based in NewYork. Constantly looking for fun collaborations and projects!
